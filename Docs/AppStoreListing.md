### Description

[DRAFT — verify before ASC]

A private, offline-first habit tracker. No accounts, no cloud, no nagging. Just
a fast, considered way to define the habits that matter, check them off each
day, and watch the streaks build.

WHY

- Local-first by default. Your data lives on your device. No servers, no
  analytics, no ads.
- Quiet by design. No streak-shaming, no upsell modals.
- Built to look good. A full design system with light, dark, and system themes
  plus a catalog of accent presets.

TRACK

- Binary habits: done or not done, one tap.
- Optional habits that do not count against you daily but can earn a weekly badge.
- Your own categories to group habits the way you think about them.

SEE THE PROGRESS

- Streaks, rings, calendars, heatmaps, and charts that read at a glance.
- Weekly goals and a Progress view that shows the trend, not just today.

PRIVACY AND OWNERSHIP

- Export your whole history to JSON anytime, import it back on a new device.
- No accounts required, ever.
